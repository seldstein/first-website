Sam Feldstein

Hi, I'm Sam Feldstein. I write fantasy and science fiction short stories, and the occasional essay. I've been doing so for about nine years now (I'm counting from college, when I began to take writing "seriously," but I've more or less been writing stories for as long as I can remember) though I've only recently begun to publish. Probably that's because it's only recently that I've written anything I consider worth publishing. You can find a list of said publications below.

For now, this website doesn't amount to much more than a portfolio. I hope to expand it, slowly but surely, to include a Writer Resources page, a Methodology page, and an About page, among other things. Beyond that, what I'd like it to be, I think, is a persona. If I add to it consistently enough for long enough, eventually it will become the digital analogue to my brain. But everything in time.

If you'd like updates about my work, the best way to get them is to subscribe to my Substack newsletter (it's free). You can also find me on Twitter, though I don't use it much, and Letterboxd. If you'd like to get in touch, you can email me at samfeldstein@proton.me.
