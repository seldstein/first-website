Sam Feldstein

Hi, I'm Sam Feldstein. I write fantasy, horror, and science fiction short stories, and the occasional essay. I've been doing so for about nine years now, though I've only recently begun to publish. You can find a list of said publications below.

For now, this website doesn't amount to much more than a portfolio. I hope to expand it, piecemeal, to include a Writer Resources page, a Methodology page, and an About page, among other things. But everything in time.

If you'd like updates about my work, the best way to get them is to subscribe to my newsletter. You can also find me on Twitter and Mastodon, though the accounts are relatively new and I can't say I use them much. If you'd like to get in touch, you can email me at samfeldstein@proton.me.
