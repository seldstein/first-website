Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu

Mindset

When I was starting out I’d worry too much about turning the tables after I got swept. I’d get overwhelmed by all the moves I couldn’t remember, all the defenses I couldn’t recall, all the submissions I didn’t know. Then my coach told me not to worry about defending or attacking. Those would come later. As a beginner, I had one job: survive.
